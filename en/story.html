IT ALL STARTED IN BERKELEY...

Alana and Jesús both arrived at UC Berkeley in the fall of 2012 to complete their MBAs. Alana had just moved to the Bay Area from Washington, DC, while Jesús came from Spain. It wasn’t long into their first semester before they met at a party – Alana was intrigued by this tall, dark, and handsome Spaniard. It turned out that the feeling was mutual. Although their first semester was hectic, Alana and Jesús found themselves hanging out more and more at their business school events.

A LOVE AFFAIR WITH GAZPACHO...

One fateful night before Thanksgiving, Jesús told Alana she should definitely go to the European Business Club Mixer the following evening, where the Spanish students would be serving delicious homemade gazpacho and tortilla española. Alana had an evening class and arrived late to the Mixer only to find that sadly all the Spanish food was gone… until Jesús saw her and smiled – he had saved some just for her!

TRAVELING IN CENTRAL AMERICA...

A few weeks later, during their school winter break, Jesús and Alana joined ~15 of their classmates on a crazy adventure traveling through Central America for 3 weeks with (mostly) no plan. While traversing from Guatemala to Costa Rica, climbing volcanos, navigating old cities, swimming in the remotest beaches, and having lots of fun, Jesús and Alana realized they were made for each other.

MANY ADVENTURES TOGETHER...

From that point on, Jesús and Alana spent more time together than apart. They discovered their mutual passion for travel, and Alana took the chance to show Jesús different parts of the US: Texas, Colorado, Hawaii, Utah...and many more. After they graduated, they both moved to Barcelona and this time Jesús took Alana to lots of new places in his country and along the Mediterranean.

SPLITTING WAYS...BUT WITH A PLAN

But like any story, there was a sad moment. At some point, Alana had to move back to the US for work, while Jesús had to stay in Barcelona. They both were heartbroken. Jesús knew he had to do something. A few days before Alana´s departure to the US, they traveled to Paris for a friend´s wedding. There, on the shores of the Seine River, he proposed to her. She said YES!

A FUTURE TOGETHER...

Alana and Jesús are now finally reunited and starting their new life together in Boston. Their time apart makes them appreciate every minute they spend together, and they are looking forward to all the adventures waiting for them ahead.
