GETTING TO THE RIVER HOUSE

We recommend that out-of-town guests fly into Austin-Bergstrom International Airport (AUS), a 50-minute drive from The River House. Alternatively, there might be good connections from certain cities to San Antonio International Airport (SAT), located 1h45 away from The River House.

We recommend that travelers rent a car to get to The River House and move around Austin. Alternatively, you can book ground transportation with SuperShuttle. Also note that ride-hailing apps Uber and Lyft are not available in the Austin area; locals use Fasten or Fare instead.

If you'd like to share a rental car with other guests, add yourself to this spreadsheet and we will help you coordinate.
